We Are Team Petsitters...

And our slogan says it all, we want to make Your Pets Our First Priority . Our aim is to take care of your pets in a professional and loving way, so that you can have peace of mind when you are away from home, either while on business, holiday, a quick break or in an emergency. We are a group of individuals who are Dedicated and Specialize in delivering a services which are of the highest standard. We will makes sure to keep you updated as well to ensure that you have the peasce of mind needed whilst you're away. One of the the benefits of making use of our service is that your pets and their Daily Routines Will Not Be Disrupted by moving them to a 2x2 kenneling or cattery facility, often found to be quite stressful and harmful to them. They will be able to carry on with their normal daily routines without being stressed out or feeling lonely. We strive to give your pets the best care and ensure that they are Safe, Comfortable and Happy in Their Natural Environment.

Overview Of Services

Our visits range from 1-2 a day and consist of putting out fresh food, water or treats in clean bowl daily. Administering of medicines, vitamins or any other special needs as required by you. Head to tail health checks. General petting, playing or letting pets out as required by you. Indoor and outdoor clean-up of pet accidents. Perimeter checks daily, arming of alarm, electricity checks, etc. Collecting of post and papers, opening closing curtains, etc. Putting lights on and off, watering of plants in and outdoors, back washing pool and adding chemicals, putting trash bin in and out. We also make sure to send daily SMS reports on pets directly to you, all with a with photo.

Limited to certain areas, we provide a live-in sleepover service from 19h00 to 07h00 the next day where sitters bring their our own food and only use your microwave ,fridge and TV. We also ensure that their are no visitors in your house unless it has been arranged with you beforehand. This service comes with all the standard services provided with a normal visit and includes a daily spot checks via our area managers to ensure standard of service is maintained throughout the stay. This service ensures that pets have someone there for them during the night and also gives you the piece of mind knowing that your house is looked after whilst you're away.

Every day between the hours fof 10h00 till 15h00 some of our staff provide a dog walking service for our furry friends who are a little overweight, bit bored, have a destructive behavior or just for general health and fitness. During this service we make sure to spend 30mins walking with your pet in your local area giving them their daily cardio and helping them hit their weight loss goal. Our sitters will make sure to play with and tire out your pets to ensure that they have a good rest afterwards. The service can also be an add on to a normal visit or sleepover stay at an additional price per day. If needed we can also send pictures through to you during the service period.
